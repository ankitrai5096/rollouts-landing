Built for real events

Not just a shared album

The details that make GroupSnap the only photo app that actually works at a party.

AI finds every photo you're in

Face recognition groups all photos of the same person automatically. Tap "Photos of me" and instantly see every candid, every group shot, every angle — without scrolling through 200 images.

Live sync — seconds, not days

The moment someone snaps a photo, it's in the shared room. Everyone sees it in real time. No waiting for people to "send them later" — because later never comes.

Original quality, always

No compression. No conversion. The full-resolution file goes straight to secure cloud storage — exactly as it came out of your phone's camera.

Private by default

Only people with the QR code or room link can see the photos. Nothing is public. Your Saturday night stays between the people who were there.

Videos too

Videos up to 20 seconds upload alongside photos. That chaotic birthday song moment, the dance floor, the speech — all of it, together.

Zero friction for guests

No account, no app install required to join. Scan the QR, upload your photos. That's it. The bar to participate is basically nothing.
